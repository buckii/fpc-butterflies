# FPC Butterflies

A collaborative educational project between Columbus School for Girls and Franklin Park Conservatory.

## About the Project

This project explores the fascinating world of butterflies through hands-on learning and scientific observation. Students engage with butterfly biology, ecology, and conservation while developing skills in research, data collection, and environmental stewardship.

## Project Goals

- **Education**: Provide students with immersive learning experiences about butterfly life cycles, habitats, and ecosystems
- **Conservation**: Raise awareness about the importance of pollinators and their role in our environment
- **Community**: Foster collaboration between educational institutions and community organizations
- **Science**: Encourage scientific inquiry and observation through real-world butterfly studies

## Partners

- **Columbus School for Girls**: An independent school dedicated to empowering young women through innovative education
- **Franklin Park Conservatory**: A botanical garden and education center in Columbus, Ohio, featuring diverse plant collections and butterfly habitats
